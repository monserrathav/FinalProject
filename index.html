History of La Carreta

La Carreta is located on 545 Hooksett Rd, Manchester, NH (AKA the better La Carreta). The first La Carreta in New Hampshire is in Nashua. This restaurant has been around for about 20 years. This is one of the many locations in New Hampshire. There are two locations in Manchester, one is in South Willow and the other one is in Hooksett Road. There is another one in Portsmouth and that has been opened for about 4 years. There is one in Derry and Londonderry. There are many owners throughout these restaurants but there is one main owner who owns the ones in Hooksett, South Willow, Nashua, and Portsmouth. She is a wonderful woman and a spectacular business woman. She is my mother's best friend and I consider her to be like my second mother. To be completly honest, Hooksett Road is my second home. My mother has worked there for about 18 years as well. My mother is a server and she is an amazing server. She is the one who taught me how to work in the food industry since I was 14 years old. There is one thing about working in the food industry, it can be so difficult to where you do not know what to do when you are busy but if you have amazing coworkers it will make your experience so much better. I feel like everyone who works at the restaurant is family even if they are not blood related. I have built so many friendships working there. I met my best friend who is like my big sister while I was working. I love my job and working there is fun but don't get it twisted it can be stressful.
